Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for delicously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 TBSP butter
  • 2 TBSP cream

Method

  1. Melt butter ina frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to coll on the plate until served)
